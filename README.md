# CivicPulse 

A browser-based city governance simulation game built for a 36-hour hackathon.

Take on the role of a city administrator and make policy decisions that affect your city's economy, environment, happiness, and infrastructure. Every choice has chain reactions — choose wisely.

## Features

- 🗳️ Policy decision system with cascading effects
- 📊 Real-time city stats (Economy, Environment, Happiness, Infrastructure)
- 🤖 AI Policy Advisor
- 🏆 Achievement system
- 🎮 Multiple difficulty modes
- 🗺️ Animated city map
- 🎵 Ambient audio

## How to Play

Just open `index.html` in your browser — no build tools, no dependencies, runs entirely in the browser.
